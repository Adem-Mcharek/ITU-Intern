About ITU Intern

ITU Intern was developed to streamline the processing of UN WebTV content into accessible, searchable transcripts with AI-powered speaker identification. This tool was created to support the International Telecommunication Union's mission of making important telecommunications meetings and discussions more accessible to global stakeholders through automated transcription and speaker diarization technology.
